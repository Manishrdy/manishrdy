About

Hello! I'm Manish, a software engineer based in San Bernardino, California, though I hail from the vibrant city of Vijayawada, India. With over three years of experience, my professional journey has been dedicated to the world of backend development and artificial intelligence. I'm passionate about building software that is not just functional, but also efficient, scalable, and capable of solving real-world challenges.

My technical experience is highlighted by my time at Yellow.Ai, where I architected a chatbot platform to handle over 100,000 daily conversations, enhancing user engagement by 35% through the practical application of NLP and LLMs. My career foundation was built upon impactful roles at Cognizant and Achiwer, where I contributed to enterprise-level microservices and full-stack e-learning platforms.

Outside of work, my lifelong passion for cricket is a constant source of inspiration, driving me to create tech projects like game simulators and predictive ML models. This same passion fuels my creative side as a freelance graphic designer, where I design cricket-related posters and stat banners. When I truly need to unwind and sharpen my focus, I find a unique sense of calm in cooking and solving LeetCode challenges.
